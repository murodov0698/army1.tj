Lesson 12: Reporting Phrases

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Objective

Students will learn and practice formal reporting phrases to communicate effectively with superiors in military contexts. By the end of the lesson, students should be able to use appropriate language when reporting incidents or requesting assistance.

Key Vocabulary

Phrase Definition Example Тарҷума
Report to the captain A direct command to go and inform a superior "All soldiers, report to the captain immediately." Ба капитан хабар диҳед
I need assistance A polite way to request help "Sir, I need assistance with this injured soldier." Ман ба кӯмак ниёз дорам
There is an emergency Reporting an urgent situation "Sir, there is an emergency at the base." Ҳолати фавқулодда ҳаст
We need reinforcements Requesting additional support "Sir, we need reinforcements at the front line." Ба мо қувваҳои иловагӣ лозим аст
Keep me updated Request for continuous information "Keep me updated on the situation." Маро дар ҷараён гузоред

Grammar Point

Formal Language

Focus on using formal language when addressing superiors:

  • Addressing: "Sir/Ma'am, __." (Ҷаноб/Хонум, __.)
  • Stating the Issue: "There is an emergency at __." (Дар __ ҳолати фавқулодда ҳаст.)
  • Requesting Action: "I need assistance with __." (Ман барои __ ба кӯмак ниёз дорам.)

Example Dialogue

Scenario: Reporting an accident during training

Soldier: "Sir, there's been an accident during training. One of the soldiers is injured."
Ҷаноб, ҳангоми машқ ҳодиса рух дод. Яке аз сарбозон ҷароҳат бардошт.
Captain: "What kind of accident? How severe are the injuries?"
Чӣ гуна ҳодиса? Ҷароҳатҳо чӣ қадар вазнинанд?
Soldier: "It was a training exercise mishap, Sir. The injuries are moderate, but we need medical assistance immediately."
Ин ҳодисаи машқи таълимӣ буд, ҷаноб. Ҷароҳатҳо миёна ҳастанд, аммо ба мо фавран ёрии тиббӣ лозим аст.
Captain: "Alright, get the medic team over there now. Keep me updated."
Хуб, ҳоло гурӯҳи тиббиро ба он ҷо фиристед. Маро дар ҷараён гузоред.

Activities

1. Vocabulary Practice

  • Word Cards: Create flashcards with reporting phrases and their definitions.
  • Partner Practice: Practice using phrases in pairs.

2. Role-Play Scenarios

  • Reporting a broken communication device
  • Informing about an injured soldier
  • Requesting reinforcements

3. Reporting Drill

Practice reporting incidents using formal language and proper phrases.

Assessment

  • Written Assessment: Write 5 reporting phrases using proper formal language
  • Participation: Engage in role-play activities and practice dialogues
