Lesson 12.Video Lesson: Reporting Phrases

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Key Points from the Video

Essential Reporting Phrases

  • "Report to the captain" (Ба капитан хабар диҳед)
    Used when directing someone to inform a superior officer
  • "I need assistance" (Ман ба кӯмак ниёз дорам)
    Formal way to request help in military situations
  • "Keep me updated" (Маро дар ҷараён гузоред)
    Used by superiors to request continuous information
  • "Sir/Ma'am" (Ҷаноб/Хонум)
    Proper way to address superior officers
  • "Situation under control" (Вазъият таҳти назорат аст)
    Used to report that an incident is being managed effectively

Example Dialogue from Video

Soldier: "Sir, I need to report an incident."
Ҷаноб, ман бояд дар бораи як ҳодиса хабар диҳам.
Officer: "Go ahead, what happened?"
Гӯед, чӣ шуд?
Soldier: "There's been an equipment malfunction in the communications room."
Дар утоқи алоқа таҷҳизот вайрон шудааст.
Officer: "I understand. Keep me updated on the situation."
Фаҳмидам. Маро аз вазъият огоҳ кунед.

Additional Notes

  • Always maintain a professional tone when reporting
  • Use clear and concise language
  • Follow the proper chain of command
  • Confirm that your message has been received and understood
