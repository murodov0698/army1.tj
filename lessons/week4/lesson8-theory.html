Lesson 8: Basic Commands

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Objective

Students will learn to understand, give, and respond to basic military commands in English, using proper imperative form and pronunciation.

Key Vocabulary

Core Command Verbs

  • Stand up
  • Sit down
  • Listen
  • Stop
  • Go
  • Come
  • Attention
  • At ease
  • Salute
  • March

Extended Vocabulary (Military Context)

  • "Fall in" (Form ranks)
  • "Dismissed" (End of duty)
  • "Report to..." (Give information)

Калимаҳои асосии фармон

  1. Истодан (Stand up)

    Мисол: "Истодан, сарбоз!" - "Stand up, soldier!"

  2. Нишастан (Sit down)

    Мисол: "Нишастан, ҳама!" - "Sit down, everyone!"

  3. Гӯш кунед (Listen)

    Мисол: "Гӯш кунед ба фармон!" - "Listen to the command!"

  4. Ист (Stop)

    Мисол: "Ист дар ҷой!" - "Stop right there!"

  5. Равед (Go)

    Мисол: "Равед ба пеш!" - "Go forward!"

  6. Биёед (Come)

    Мисол: "Биёед ба ин ҷо!" - "Come here!"

  7. Диққат (Attention)

    Мисол: "Диққат, рост истодан!" - "Attention, stand straight!"

  8. Озод (At ease)

    Мисол: "Озод, сарбозон!" - "At ease, soldiers!"

  9. Салом диҳед (Salute)

    Мисол: "Салом диҳед ба афсар!" - "Salute the officer!"

  10. Қадам занед (March)

    Мисол: "Қадам занед ба пеш!" - "March forward!"

Grammar Point: Imperative Form (Шакли амрӣ)

Rules:

  1. Base verb only for affirmative commands:
    "Stand up."
    "Listen carefully."
  2. "Do not" + base verb for negative commands:
    "Do not move."
  3. Adding politeness/urgency:
    "Immediately stand at attention!"
    "Quickly report to the captain!"

Activities

1. Command Chains (15 mins)

Task: Students form lines. The first student whispers a command (e.g., "Salute") to the next, who executes it. The last student says the command aloud.

Goal: Practice listening and execution.

2. Drill Sergeant Role-Play (20 mins)

Scenario: One student acts as an officer, the other as a recruit.

Sample Dialogue:
Officer: "Private, stand at attention!"
Recruit: (Executes action) "Yes, sir!"
Officer: "March to the door!"

3. Scenario-Based Command Creation (25 mins)

Groups invent commands for:

  • Morning inspection ("Line up alphabetically!")
  • Weapon cleaning ("Inspect your barrel!")
  • Emergency evacuation ("Exit in single file!")

Tajik Translations for Key Commands

English Tajik
"Stand up!" "Истодан!"
"Sit down!" "Нишастан!"
"Listen!" "Гӯш кунед!"
"Stop!" "Ист!"

Teaching Notes

  • Emphasize tone: Commands should be loud, clear, and authoritative.
  • Use physical demonstrations for clarity.
  • Repeat drills until responses become automatic.
