Lesson 14. Video Lesson: Relationship Phrases

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Key Points from the Video

Essential Relationship Vocabulary

  • "Friend" (FREND)
    Дӯст - Used to refer to a close companion
  • "Colleague" (KOL-ee-g)
    Ҳамкор - Used to refer to someone you work with
  • "Comrade" (KOM-rayd)
    Ҳамрада - Used to refer to a fellow soldier
  • "Squad Leader" (SKWOD LEED-ur)
    Сардаста - Used to refer to the leader of a squad
  • "Fellow Soldier" (FEL-oh SOL-jur)
    Ҳамрадаи ҳарбӣ - Used to refer to another soldier in the same unit

Example Dialogue from Video

Soldier A: "This is my squad leader, Captain Smith."
"Ин сардастаи ман, Капитан Смит аст."
Soldier B: "Nice to meet you, Captain. I'm his fellow soldier."
"Хушбахтам, Капитан. Ман ҳамрадаи ҳарбии Ӯ ҳастам."
Captain: "Welcome to the unit. We're all comrades here."
"Хуш омадед ба воҳид. Мо ҳама ҳамрада ҳастем."

Additional Notes

  • Always use appropriate relationship terms in military contexts
  • Practice pronunciation of each relationship word carefully
  • Learn to use these words in complete sentences
  • Remember to use formal language when talking about relationships in professional settings
