Lesson 13: Family Vocabulary

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Objective

Learn and practice vocabulary related to family members, understand how to use possessive pronouns correctly, and apply the vocabulary in simple sentences and conversations about your family.

Омӯзиш ва машқи луғатҳои оила, фаҳмидани истифодаи замири иёфӣ ва истифодаи луғатҳо дар ҷумлаҳои сода ва муколамаҳо дар бораи оилаи худ.

Key Vocabulary

Word Pronunciation Example Тарҷума
Father FAH-thur "My father is a teacher." Падар
Mother MUH-thur "Her mother works at a hospital." Модар
Brother BROH-thur "I have two brothers." Бародар
Sister SIS-tur "My sister is younger than me." Хохар
Son SON "Their son is in school." Писар
Daughter DAW-tur "His daughter loves to read." Духтар
Grandfather GRAND-fahthur "My grandfather tells great stories." Бобо
Grandmother GRAND-muhthur "Her grandmother makes delicious food." Биби
Aunt ANT "My aunt lives in another city." Амма
Uncle UHNK-ul "His uncle is a doctor." Амак

Grammar Point

Possessive Pronouns

Learn how to use possessive pronouns to describe family members:

  • My - "My father works as an engineer." (Падарам муҳандис аст.)
  • Your - "Your brother is very tall." (Бародарат бисёр баланд аст.)
  • His - "His sister loves music." (Хоҳараш мусиқиро дӯст медорад.)
  • Her - "Her mother is a nurse." (Модараш духтури хамшира аст.)
  • Our - "Our grandmother lives with us." (Бибиямон бо мо зиндагӣ мекунад.)
  • Their - "Their daughter is my friend." (Духтарашон дӯсти ман аст.)

Activities

1. Describe Your Family

Write or tell a short description of your family using the new vocabulary:

"I have a small family. My father is a teacher, and my mother is a nurse. I have one sister, and we live with our grandmother."
"Ман оилаи хурд дорам. Падарам муаллим аст ва модарам духтури хамшира аст. Ман як хоҳар дорам ва мо бо бибиямон зиндагӣ мекунем."

2. Role-Play: Introduce Your Family

Practice these questions with a partner:

  • "Do you have any brothers or sisters?" (Оё шумо бародар ё хоҳар доред?)
  • "What does your father do?" (Падарат чӣ кор мекунад?)
  • "Where does your grandmother live?" (Бибият дар куҷо зиндагӣ мекунад?)

3. Create a Family Tree

Draw a simple family tree and label each member using the vocabulary you've learned.

Шаҷараи оилаи сода кашед ва ҳар як аъзоро бо луғатҳои омӯхташуда номгузорӣ кунед.

Homework

  • Bring a photo of your family to class and describe each member.
  • Write a short paragraph about your family using possessive pronouns and the new vocabulary.

Assessment

  • Oral Assessment: Participation in role-play and family descriptions
  • Written Assessment: Sentences and paragraphs about your family
