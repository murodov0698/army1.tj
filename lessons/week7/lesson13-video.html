Lesson 13. Video Lesson: Family Vocabulary

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Key Points from the Video

Essential Family Vocabulary

  • "Father" (FAH-thur)
    Падар - Used to refer to one's male parent
  • "Mother" (MUH-thur)
    Модар - Used to refer to one's female parent
  • "Brother" (BROH-thur)
    Бародар - Used to refer to one's male sibling
  • "Sister" (SIS-tur)
    Хохар - Used to refer to one's female sibling
  • "Grandfather" (GRAND-fahthur)
    Бобо - Used to refer to one's father's or mother's father
  • "Grandmother" (GRAND-muhthur)
    Биби - Used to refer to one's father's or mother's mother

Example Dialogue from Video

Student: "This is my family photo. Let me introduce you to everyone."
"Ин акси оилаи ман аст. Бигзор ман шуморо бо ҳама муаррифӣ кунам."
Teacher: "Yes, please tell us about your family."
"Бале, лутфан дар бораи оилаи худ ба мо бигӯед."
Student: "This is my father. He's a teacher. And this is my mother. She works at a hospital."
"Ин падарам аст. Ӯ муаллим аст. Ва ин модарам аст. Ӯ дар беморхона кор мекунад."
Teacher: "Do you have any brothers or sisters?"
"Оё шумо бародар ё хоҳар доред?"

Additional Notes

  • Always use appropriate possessive pronouns when talking about family members
  • Practice pronunciation of each family member word carefully
  • Learn to use these words in complete sentences
  • Remember to use formal language when talking about family in professional settings
