Lesson 17: Weather Vocabulary

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Objective

In this lesson, students will learn and practice vocabulary related to weather conditions. They will understand the importance of weather in various military operations and develop the ability to describe weather accurately in both written and spoken English.

Дар ин дарс донишҷӯён луғати марбут ба ҳолатҳои обу ҳаворо меомӯзанд ва машқ мекунанд. Онҳо аҳамияти обу ҳаворо дар амалиётҳои гуногуни ҳарбӣ мефаҳманд ва қобилияти тавсифи дақиқи обу ҳаворо ҳам дар забони навишта ва ҳам дар забони гуфтугӯӣ пайдо мекунанд.

Key Vocabulary

Word Definition Example Тарҷума
Sunny Weather with clear skies and sunshine "The sunny weather made it easier for the soldiers to navigate." Офтобӣ - Ҳаво бо осмони равшан ва офтоб
Rainy Weather with precipitation in the form of rain "The rainy conditions made the terrain slippery." Боронӣ - Ҳаво бо борон
Windy Weather with strong and steady air flow "The windy conditions interfered with the sniper's shots." Шамолӣ - Ҳаво бо шамоли қавӣ
Cold Weather with low temperatures "The cold weather required additional layers of clothing." Хунук - Ҳаво бо ҳарорати паст
Hot Weather with high temperatures "The hot weather caused dehydration among the soldiers." Гарм - Ҳаво бо ҳарорати баланд
Cloudy Weather with clouds in the sky "The cloudy weather provided cover for the soldiers." Абрнок - Ҳаво бо абрҳо дар осмон
Snowy Weather with falling snow "The snowy conditions made movement difficult." Барфӣ - Ҳаво бо борон ва барф
Foggy Weather with thick cloud of water droplets "The foggy weather reduced visibility." Туманӣ - Ҳаво бо туман
Stormy Weather with severe conditions "The stormy weather forced the soldiers to take shelter." Тӯфонӣ - Ҳаво бо шароити вазнин
Clear Weather with cloudless sky "The clear weather provided perfect conditions for night operations." Равшан - Ҳаво бо осмони беабр

Grammar Point

Describing the Weather

Learn how to describe weather conditions using these structures:

  • "It is sunny." ("Ҳаво офтобӣ аст.")
  • "The weather is rainy." ("Ҳаво боронӣ аст.")
  • "We are experiencing a storm." ("Мо тӯфонро аз сар мегузаронем.")
  • "What is the weather like?" ("Ҳаво чӣ гуна аст?")
  • "The weather will be hot tomorrow." ("Фардо ҳаво гарм хоҳад буд.")

Activities

1. Vocabulary Practice

Match each vocabulary word with its definition and discuss weather impact:

"Why is it important to know if the weather is sunny or rainy?" ("Чаро муҳим аст, ки бидонем ҳаво офтобӣ ё боронӣ аст?")

2. Role-Play

Practice weather reporting and mission planning:

Weather Reporter: "The weather is stormy with heavy rain." ("Ҳаво тӯфонӣ бо борони зиёд аст.")
Mission Planner: "We need to postpone the mission." ("Мо бояд миссияро баъдтар гузаронем.")

3. Scenario-Based Practice

Create a simulated military scenario with weather conditions:

"A group of soldiers is planning a nighttime patrol in foggy weather." ("Гурӯҳе аз сарбозон ҳангоми ҳавои туманӣ барои гаштугузор шабӣ тайёрӣ мебинанд.")

Homework

  • Write a paragraph describing weather conditions for a mission
  • Create vocabulary flashcards
  • Research weather impact on military operations

Assessment

  • Oral Assessment: Role-play weather reporting scenarios
  • Written Assessment: Weather report for a military operation
