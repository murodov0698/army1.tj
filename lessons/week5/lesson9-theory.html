Дарси 9: Telling Time

Назария Машқҳо Видео

Objective

Students will learn to tell time in English using both analog and digital formats, ask and answer time-related questions, and describe daily schedules with proper time expressions.

I. Key Vocabulary

A. Time Expressions

  1. Full hours: "o'clock" (3 o'clock) - соат се
  2. Half hours: "half past" (half past 5) - соат панҷу ним
  3. Quarter hours:
    • "quarter past" (quarter past 2) - соат дую понздаҳ дақиқа
    • "quarter to" (quarter to 9) - соат нӯҳ кам понздаҳ дақиқа
  4. Minutes:
    • "ten past seven" (7:10) - соат ҳафту даҳ дақиқа
    • "twenty to four" (3:40) - соат чор кам бист дақиқа

B. Numbers Review (1-60)

Tricky numbers:

  • 12 (twelve) - дувоздаҳ
  • 13 (thirteen) - сездаҳ
  • 15 (fifteen) - понздаҳ
  • 20 (twenty) - бист
  • 30 (thirty) - сӣ
  • 40 (forty) - чил
  • 50 (fifty) - панҷоҳ

II. Grammar Points

A. Asking for the Time

  1. Formal: "Excuse me, what time is it?" - Бубахшед, соат чанд?
  2. Informal: "What's the time?" - Соат чанд?
  3. Alternative: "Do you have the time?" - Соатро медонед?

B. Telling the Time

  1. Digital format:
    • "It's seven fifteen" (7:15) - Соат ҳафту понздаҳ дақиқа
    • "It's fourteen thirty" (14:30) - Соат чордаҳу сӣ дақиқа
  2. Analog format:
    • "It's quarter past two" (2:15) - Соат дую понздаҳ дақиқа
    • "It's ten to nine" (8:50) - Соат нӯҳ кам даҳ дақиқа

Common Errors & Corrections

  1. "It's seven fifteen o'clock" → Remove "o'clock"
  2. "It's half to five" → "half past four"
  3. Confusing AM/PM → Use "morning/afternoon" initially
  4. Military time errors → Drill "1300 = 1 PM"
